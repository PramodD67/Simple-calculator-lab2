Privacy Policy

SQL Joins PlayGround provides SQL join practice content through an in-app web experience.

Information We Collect

If you opt in, the app collects anonymous usage analytics through Firebase Analytics, including app opens, approximate device information, and general engagement events. This information is used to understand usage trends and improve app quality.

What We Do Not Collect

This app does not request your contacts, microphone, camera, precise location, SMS data, or other sensitive device permissions for its core functionality.

Your Choices

On first launch, you can choose whether to allow anonymous analytics collection. If you decline, analytics collection remains disabled unless you reinstall the app and opt in later.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Firebase Analytics, a Google service. Review Google's Firebase and Google Analytics documentation for more information about how analytics data is handled.

Internet Access

The app requires an internet connection to load SQL learning content from the app website.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information provided on this app's Google Play listing.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or service updates.
